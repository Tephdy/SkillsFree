Scholarship Programs at edX

The Macquarie Group Scholarship Program

The Macquarie Group Scholarship Program: In the wake of COVID-19, Macquarie Group has established this scholarship to support economic recovery by assisting learners seeking skills-based programming on edX. Preference will be given learners from low-income and underrepresented groups in Australia, India, the United Kingdom, and the United States. Macquarie Group Scholarships cover 100% of the cost of any edX course for the learner.
